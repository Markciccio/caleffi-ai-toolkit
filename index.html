Botpress: Intelligent Customer Support

What is it for?

Create AI chatbots for multilingual customer support and internal assistance, using the company's knowledge base (price lists, FAQs, etc.).

Caleffi Quick Guide:

  • Initial Setup: Access Botpress, create a new bot.
  • Key Instructions (Personality): 
    You are a customer assistant for Caleffi... (insert your full instructions here)
  • Knowledge Base: Upload relevant PDF and TXT documents (e.g., `2025 Price List`, `Product FAQs`).
  • Test: Use the emulator to check responses.
Caleffi Tip: Keep the knowledge base updated and test the bot regularly with real questions to ensure accurate and helpful answers. Never mention the KB file names.

DeepL: Professional Translations

What is it for?

Translate texts and documents (PDF, Word, PowerPoint) with high quality, maintaining formatting and using custom glossaries for Caleffi terminology.

Caleffi Quick Guide:

  • Access: Go to DeepL Translator.
  • File Translation: Select "Translate files", upload the document, choose languages.
  • Glossaries: Create and use glossaries for Caleffi-specific technical terms (e.g., "Valvola di zona" -> "Zone Valve"). This is crucial!
Caleffi Tip: A well-maintained glossary is key for consistent and professional technical translations. Always review important translated documents.

D-ID: AI Avatar Videos and Video Translations

What is it for?

Create videos with AI talking avatars and translate existing videos (e.g., tutorials, presentations) into different languages using the "Video Translate" feature.

Caleffi Quick Guide:

  • Access: Register or log in to D-ID Studio.
  • Video Translate: Upload your video, select the target language and voice.
  • Avatar Creation: You can create videos from scratch with text and a default or uploaded avatar.
Caleffi Tip: Great for quickly making video content available to all branches. Check lip-sync and the naturalness of the AI voice.

Google Gemini: Generative AI and Gems

What is it for?

Generate texts, analyze documents, create custom assistants ("Gems") for specific tasks like drafting technical specifications or technical support, and generate audio.

Caleffi Quick Guide:

  • Access: Go to Gemini.
  • Creating Gems:
    • Click on "Create a new Gem" (or similar).
    • Name: E.g., "Caleffi Specifications Solver".
    • Instructions: "You are an assistant for drafting Caleffi technical specifications..."
    • Knowledge: Upload relevant PDFs and documents (e.g., Caleffi product lists for the "Specifications Solver").
  • Caleffi Gem Examples: "Specifications Solver", "Specification Analysis", "Caleffi Technical Assistant".
Caleffi Tip: Define clear instructions and provide relevant knowledge documents to get the most out of your Gems. Experiment with prompts!

Helpful Tips

Ethics and Data Security

NEVER upload confidential company information, sensitive personal data, or trade secrets to public AI tools not formally approved by Caleffi for such purposes. Be aware of how data is handled.

Prompt Engineering

To get better results from generative AIs (like Gemini), learn to write clear, specific, and detailed "prompts" (instructions). Provide context and define the desired outcome.

Always Verify

AI is powerful, but not infallible. Always verify the information generated, especially for technical documents, official communications, or important decisions. AI is an assistant, not a substitute for expert human judgment.

Stay Updated

The AI field evolves rapidly. Revisit this Toolkit and follow internal communications to discover new tools or updates on Caleffi best practices.
